Illusion Image Classification¶

This notebook is largely based on this tutorial from fast.ai

What it aims to do is classify a person as a celebrity or non-celebrity based on a picture of someone's face. How I tried to do this is by scraping images from Google of celebrities & non-celebrities and running a CNN on these using fastai.vision.

The results indicate that it is not very effective at classifying a celebrity as a celebrity, although it is stronger at classifying non-celebrities as non-celebrities. It has a roughly 20% error-rate as opposed to a 50% error on binary classification. That small bit of classification strength seems to me to be most likely due to differences in the types of photos and the look of the people. Celebrities have a lot more make-up, wear nicer clothes and have more professional photos.
